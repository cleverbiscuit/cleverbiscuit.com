About Us

Clever Biscuit Ltd is a privately held company founded in 2011 in the heart of England. We make simple, innovative and useful products.

We’re a young company driven by passion and bucket loads of caffeine. What the team lacks in size, we make up for in creativity and hard-work. We believe in combining different disciplines to produce great ideas that make people's lives better.

By bringing together our experiences and skills, we aim to push the boundaries of our individual abilities to build something rather good. The name Clever Biscuit was chosen in honour of Maebellyne’s amazing biscotti recipe, which have kept us all going through some late nights.
